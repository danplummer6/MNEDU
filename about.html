About Minnesota Educational Tours

MN Educational Tours is a division of Erving Aviation that specializes in providing group travel. Most of our customer base consists of collegiate sports teams, corporate clients and leisure groups.

Over the years we received a lot of feedback from teachers and parents regarding the high cost of a typical 3 day educational trip to Washington DC. In an effort to create an affordable option, MN Educational Tours was born!
