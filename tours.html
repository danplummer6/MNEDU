About Our Tours

Trips will be available September-November and April-May, Monday thru Thursday based on availability. Participants will depart from Minneapolis Humphrey Terminal 2 at 6:00 am, arriving in Washington at 9:25 am. Three motor coaches with licensed tour guides and WIFI will be waiting upon your arrival.

Each school can create a custom day of tour stops but a recommended day would include a White House photo stop, Arlington National Cemetery/Changing of the Guards, US Capital Building, Washington Monument, World War II Memorial, Lincoln Memorial, Vietnam Veterans Memorial, Korean War Memorial, Jefferson Memorial and a museum stop, (Smithsonian, National Air and Space). You can either choose to fit a few more stops in your day then have dinner on the aircraft or enjoy a wonderful dinner at the Great American Steak Buffet.

The busses will drop you back off at the airport at 7:30 for an 8:40 departure, arriving back to MSP at 10:10 pm the same day. Breakfast will be served on the airplane, the tour will stop midday for a catered lunch and each group can decide to either have dinner in Washington or on the aircraft. Price is $480 per person, based on 158 passengers, this includes all taxes and all fees. Three teachers and or chaperones will be allowed to travel free of charge making your total group size 161.
