Minnesota Educational Tours

MN Educational Tours specializes in educational day trips from Minneapolis to Washington DC. From tour stops to dinner, each group can customize their experience. With our extensive relationships in the travel industry we provide the best services at an economical price. So let us take care of all the details so you can focus on having fun!

Mission

Our mission is to provide low cost, personally planned, educational day trips, for school groups.

Values

We value an experience that:

  • Is both educational and filled with great memories.
  • Provides great features at an economical price.
  • Offers options to adapt the trip for each group.
  • Takes care of the details for your enjoyment.

Vision

Our vision is to expand education in Minnesota beyond the classroom by learning through experience.
